Hello! I'm Jo-Anne, a full-stack web developer residing in a lovely town called Klang, located in a tropical country known as Malaysia.

I graduated from Monash University in 2016 with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Computer Systems Engineering and worked in the field of industrial automation for several years. During this time, I can't help the feeling of wanting to impact this world more directly, but not knowing how to.

I decided to participate in several volunteer activities, but something was still missing. It wasn't until I joined a German company in industrial automation as a salesperson, and was given the chance to participate in a series of programming training sessions, that I finally realised what was missing.

In pursuit of this feverish passion I once experienced in programming classes back in my degree, I did a quick research on the job market for software developers, then decided to take a leap of faith and persue a 3-month full-time bootcamp in full-stack web development.

Here I am, 3 months later with a belt of shiny, newly acquired skills along with newfound confidence, passion and excitement!

My list of ever-growing interests includes: modern single-page-apps, mobile apps, software development, big data analytics, artificial intelligence and machine learning.
