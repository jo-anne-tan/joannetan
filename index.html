That means I work with front-end - the pages you see and interact with on a browser - and the backend server that drives it

Hello! I'm Jo-Anne, a full-stack web developer residing in Klang, a lovely town in a tropical country known as Malaysia.

I graduated from Monash University in 2016 with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Computer Systems Engineering and worked in the industrial automation industry for several years. During this time, I felt a strong need to impact this world more directly, but not knowing how to.

I participated in volunteer activities with NGOs. It was fun, but something was missing. It wasn't until I participated in the programming training sessions in my previous company that I finally realised what that was.

In pursuit of the feverish passion I once experienced in university programming assignments, I did a quick research on the job market for software developers and decided to take a leap of faith and persue a 3-month bootcamp in full-stack web development.

Here I am, 3 months later with a belt of shiny new skills alongside newfound confidence, passion and excitement!
